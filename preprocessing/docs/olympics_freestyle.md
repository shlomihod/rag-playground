# Freestyle skiing at the 2026 Winter Olympics

Freestyle skiing at the 2026 Winter Olympics is held at the Livigno Snow Park and Livigno Aerials & Moguls Park in Valtellina, Italy. The events are held between 7 and 21 February 2026. A total of 15 freestyle skiing events are held, up from 13 in 2022. Dual moguls events are making Olympic debut.
