# Biathlon at the 2026 Winter Olympics

Biathlon at the 2026 Winter Olympics will be held at the Anterselva Biathlon Arena in Rasen-Antholz from 8 to 21 February 2026.
A total of 11 events will be contested, five for men, five for women and one mixed.
