# Cross-country skiing at the 2026 Winter Olympics

Cross-country skiing at the 2026 Winter Olympics will be held at the Cross country and biathlon center Fabio Canal in Tesero, Italy.
A total of 12 events will be contested, six each for men and women. For the first time, women will race the same distances as men.
