# Short track speed skating at the 2026 Winter Olympics

Short-track speed skating at the 2026 Winter Olympics will be held at the Milano Ice Skating Arena in Milan, Italy. The races are scheduled to take place between 10 and 20 February 2026. A total of nine short-track speed skating competitions will be held, the same as in 2022. Also the number of quota spots, a total of 112 stayed the same.
